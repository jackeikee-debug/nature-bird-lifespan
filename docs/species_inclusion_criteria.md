# Species Inclusion Criteria

Prioritize species that have:

1. Maximum lifespan and body mass in AnAge.
2. A public genome assembly and usable protein annotation.
3. Clear flight status and broad ecological labels.
4. Coverage across birds, bats, non-flying mammals, reptiles, and anchor models.

Exclude or defer species when:

1. Lifespan records are absent or clearly anecdotal.
2. Taxonomy cannot be reconciled across lifespan, genome, and phylogeny sources.
3. Genome annotation is too fragmented for ortholog or pathway-score work.
